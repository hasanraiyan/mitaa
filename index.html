Our Purpose

Connecting MIT's Past, Present & Future

The Muzaffarpur Institute of Technology Alumni Association (MITAA) exists to cultivate a vibrant, lifelong community for our graduates. We champion connections, provide valuable resources, celebrate successes, and actively support the advancement of our alma mater and its students.
